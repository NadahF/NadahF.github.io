How can I add friends?

If you navigate to the "Groups" page above, you can click on a specific friend to add a group to add a friend to. You can add a friend based on their email or you can scan the QR code from their app and they will be automatically added to a group that you sepcify.

How can I turn off my location?

You can turn off your location by navigating to your profile page and checking the box that will turn off your locations and you will no longer be visible on the map for any friends you have on the application.
